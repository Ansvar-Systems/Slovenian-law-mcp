# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent and discuss technical disagreements in good faith
- Focus feedback on code, decisions, and outcomes, not individuals
- Help keep collaboration clear and professional

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct
- Personal attacks, intimidation, or sustained hostility
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- Deliberate disruption of project collaboration

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to:

- `hello@ansvar.ai`

All reports are handled confidentially to the extent possible.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take appropriate action for violations, including warning, temporary restrictions, or removal from participation spaces.
